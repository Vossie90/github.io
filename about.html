Cafe t Vossie is a traditional dutch pub located in Amsterdam-West. Daniel Vosveld Started Cafe 't Vossie in early 2012. his mission is to keep the old brown dutch bar concept alive for the locals in Amsterdam We have a beautiful beautiful terrace in the neighbourhood of the baarsjes. you can watch:

  • Football
  • American Football
  • Darts
  • Rugby
you can play:
  • Pool
  • Darts
  • Chess
  • Various boardgames
  • Various Cardgames
Feel free to come by and visit soon! p.s. before you do, check our Menu. we probably have something interesting you want to try!
