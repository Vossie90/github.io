Cafe t'Vossie is a traditional Dutch pub located in Amsterdam-West. Started by Daniel Vosveld in early 2012, his mission is to keep the classic brown Dutch cafe concept alive for the locals in Amsterdam. We have a beautiful terrace in the heart of the Baarsjes neighborhood.
